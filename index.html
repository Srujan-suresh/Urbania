Any Questions?

How do I get started with booking an Urbania?

You can simply choose your preferred Urbania model (10-seater or 16-seater), select the travel date, share your pickup location, and confirm your booking. The process is quick and hassle-free.

Can I book an Urbania with a debit card?

Yes, most bookings can be secured using a debit card or UPI payment. We also support wallet and bank transfer options.

Which type of Urbania should I choose?

If you have a larger group, the 16-seater Urbania is ideal. For smaller groups or family travel, the 10-seater Urbania offers perfect comfort.

Is there a security deposit for Urbania rentals?

Yes, a standard refundable security deposit may be required, depending on the booking type, distance, and travel plan.

Can I cancel or modify my Urbania reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking based on our cancellation policy. We recommend informing us as early as possible for smooth adjustments.

Can I extend my Urbania rental period?

Absolutely! You can extend your rental by contacting our support team. Extensions depend on vehicle availability for your new dates.
